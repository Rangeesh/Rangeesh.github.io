An individual with a passion for problem solving and consistent learning is whom I see on the mirror daily. My journey at IIT Madras and Texas A&M has imbibed an avid interest in automobiles and robotics in me, and therefore I have decided to explore the field of autonomous vehicles and have found it to be extremely enthralling.

I have worked with competitive teams since my sophomore year, competing in SAE organised international events held in Asia and in Europe, and most recently, in USA. It has been an incredible experience to compete with the best teams from different parts of the world and learn a lot at the same time too. Having to deal with tight deadlines and high pressure situations, these amazing experiences have built the person that I am today. Not taking away any credit from my teammates, any achievements relating to these competitions have been the joint effort of everyone involved, and their companionship has made the whole journey more enjoyable.

More recently, I have had a taste of leading and teaching groups of students and making sure that while the students enjoy the class, they have learnt important conceptual things at the end of the day, whether it be a technical or a life skill.

Key takeaways from these would be that I have developed a good work ethic and have an ever improving time management strategy that has led me to achieve more in a shorter span of time. I'm eagerly looking forward towards the next step in my career to join a great team that is highly motivated to achieve what they aim for!
